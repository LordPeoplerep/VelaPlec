About VelaPlex Systems

VelaPlex Systems is the software and AI division of the Velakoritsu Group (Ticker: VLK), specializing in advanced AI models, blockchain-secured databases, custom programming languages, and secure infrastructure for a wide range of industries.

Our flagship AI — VELA — supports multi-model operation with APMs, HLMs, and AKMs for advanced reasoning, adaptability, and unmatched performance across industries.

Products

APM Series

Advanced Processor Models (APM) drive the intelligence of VELA. Current model: APM-4; APM-5 is in development. High reasoning, multi-model switching, and exceptional accuracy.

Peoplerep OS

Our proprietary OS integrating hardware and AI for efficiency, security, and adaptability. Peoplerep OS 3 in use; OS 4 in development with advanced multi-language support.

VELA Chat Models

ALLMs (Advanced Logic Language Models) including APMs, HLMs, AKMs. High reasoning AI models for tailored performance.

Streamora
